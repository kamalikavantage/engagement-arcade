📰 Company Culture Story

This month, our organization demonstrated stronger collaboration and engagement across departments. Recognition activity reached an all-time high with 4,280 recognitions exchanged, signaling a healthier recognition culture.

Cross-department connections strengthened significantly. Marketing and Sales exchanged recognitions at rates 34% higher than historical averages, reflecting our improved alignment on customer-focused initiatives. Engineering continued to lead in peer recognition and innovation-themed acknowledgments.

Core values remain strong. Collaboration emerged as the top-recognized value (42% of all recognitions), followed by Leadership and Innovation. This reflects our organizational focus on teamwork and forward-thinking solutions.

Looking ahead, we have an opportunity to reinvigorate manager recognition programs and re-engage the Finance team. By building on the momentum we've established, we can continue strengthening our recognition culture and reinforcing the behaviors that drive our success.
